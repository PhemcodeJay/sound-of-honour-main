Our Story & Vision

Founded in 2019, Sound of Honour Global Ministry began with a divine mandate to raise a generation of true worshippers who honour God in spirit and truth.

What started as a small community outreach during the COVID-19 pandemic has grown into a multifaceted ministry dedicated to transforming lives through worship, prayer, and compassionate action.

Our mission extends beyond the walls of the church, reaching into communities with practical support, spiritual guidance, and the life-changing message of the Gospel.
