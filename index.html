Our Journey & Vision

Founded in Faith – Born in Crisis

Sound of Honour Global Services was birthed in 2019 during the global COVID-19 pandemic. What began as door-to-door evangelism and feeding programs for children has grown into a vibrant ministry focused on worship, music education, spiritual empowerment, and community transformation.

Through the power of music, prayer, and compassionate action, we are raising a generation that honours God and serves humanity with excellence and love.
