Welcome to Sound of Honour Global Ministry

Founded in 2019 during the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, our ministry began with door-to-door evangelism and feeding programs for children. Guided by God's vision, we discovered the power of music and worship as a tool for healing, encouragement, and spiritual growth.

Today, we dedicate ourselves to Christian worship, community empowerment, music education, and gospel production. Through music, prayer, and compassionate action, we aim to uplift lives, strengthen faith, and bring hope to those in need.

"Through music, prayer, and compassionate action, we aim to uplift lives, strengthen faith, and bring hope to those in need."

Our Mission Focus

Heartfelt Worship Experiences

Leading powerful worship services that connect people with God's presence and bring spiritual renewal.

Intercessory Prayer Ministry

Praying for individuals, families, and communities for breakthrough, healing, and divine intervention.

Community Support Programs

Providing school fees, rent assistance, and basic needs for orphans and the underprivileged.

Guidance & Mentorship

Offering counseling and mentorship for couples and young adults seeking godly direction.
