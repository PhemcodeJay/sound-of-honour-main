Our Ministry Journey

Welcome to Sound Of Honour Global Services

Founded in 2019 during the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, our ministry began with a calling to serve through door-to-door evangelism and feeding programs for children. Guided by God's vision, we discovered the power of music and worship as a tool for healing, encouragement, and spiritual growth.

Today, we dedicate ourselves to Christian worship, community empowerment, music education, and gospel production. Through music, prayer, and compassionate action, we aim to uplift lives, strengthen faith, and bring hope to those in need.

Our Mission Focus

  • Heartfelt Worship Experiences
  • Intercessory Prayer Ministry
  • Community Support Programs
  • Guidance & Mentorship
